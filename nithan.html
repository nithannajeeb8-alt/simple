The Journey So Far

Curiosity & Creation.

My name is Nithan Najeeb. I was born in Kerala in 2004. I am not looking to follow a conventional path. I just want to build a life around curiosity, creativity, and continuous growth.

01 — The Search

Beyond the Textbooks.

For much of my life, I have been searching for work that I genuinely enjoy and find meaningful. That search pulled me into online business early on. It gave me practical knowledge of commerce and showed me how things actually operate in the real world, far beyond what you can learn in a classroom.

02 — Purpose

Building Remevess.

I wanted to create something with real purpose. This led me to start Remevess, a venture focused on promoting traditional handmade products and supporting skilled craftsmen so they could earn a fair income. Though I ran it for only a few months, it taught me invaluable lessons about entrepreneurship, people, and the true value of work.

03 — Structure

Finding Balance.

Over time, I discovered a growing interest in how the foundation of a business holds together. I developed a deep appreciation for accounting and decided to pursue a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) with a specialization in Computer Applications. It bridges two things I love: the logic of numbers and the boundless potential of technology.

04 — The Present

The Creator.

Beyond academics and business, I am a creator. Whether I am writing stories, building minimal websites, or designing applications, the motivation is always the same. I am constantly driven by the desire to learn, to create, and to contribute something meaningful to the world.
