Nithan Najeeb.

Born in Kerala in 2004, I am currently pursuing a Bachelor of Commerce (B.Com) with a specialization in Computer Applications. I am not looking to follow a conventional path. I am building a life around curiosity, creativity, and continuous growth.

The Journey of Nithan Najeeb

01 — The Search

Beyond the Textbooks.

Much of my life has been spent searching for work that I genuinely enjoy. That search pulled me into online business early on, giving me practical knowledge of commerce. I learned how things actually operate in the real world, bypassing theory for true mechanics.

02 — Purpose

Building Remevess.

I wanted to create something with real purpose. This led me to start Remevess, a venture focused on promoting traditional handmade products and supporting skilled craftsmen. Though it ran briefly, it taught me invaluable lessons about entrepreneurship, people, and the true value of work.

03 — Structure

Commerce & Code.

I discovered a growing interest in how the foundation of a business holds together. I developed a deep appreciation for accounting and pursued a B.Com in Computer Applications. It beautifully bridges two things I love: the logic of numbers and the boundless potential of software technology.

04 — The Present

The Creator.

Beyond academics and business, I am a creator. Whether I am writing stories, building minimalist websites, or designing applications, the motivation is always the same. I am driven by the desire to learn, create, and contribute something meaningful to the web.
